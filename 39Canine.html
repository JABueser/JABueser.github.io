39 Canine

Online shopping for genetic test kits for dogs.

Background

39Canine was conceived through the importance and love of your dog. We got the idea from the company of 23andMe, which is a genetics and ancestry company for humans, which we studied and did a case study report on.

We took the fundamental idea of 23andMe, and converted it onto another important aspect of our lives: our pets. We offer a full genetic and health test for your beloved doggie, that way you have all the tools in order to provide a happy and healthy life for your four legged family member.

​

Value for our Customers

Our product is cheap and reliable since we use Performagene™ as our DNA kit provider.​

We keep our prices low; we’re HALF the cost of our competitors. One click and you’re on the way to unraveling the mystery of your dog’s DNA – because your dog is more than just a pet…he’s a member of the family.​

E-Business Mission

Our mission is to bring together dog owners and dogs by providing data. This information tells dog owners about their dog’s genetic heritage, mix and health.
